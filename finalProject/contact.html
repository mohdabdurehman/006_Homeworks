Common Questions

Do you offer personal training or coaching?

Not formally — this is a personal passion project. But I'm happy to answer questions and point you in the right direction through the contact form above.

Are the workout programs suitable for beginners?

Yes — the Full Body Blast routine is specifically designed for those new to lifting. The others can be scaled by adjusting weight and rest periods to match your level.

Can I use the meal plan if I'm vegetarian?

Absolutely. Swap chicken and fish for tofu, tempeh, legumes, cottage cheese, eggs, and Greek yoghurt. The macro targets remain the same — just adjust your protein sources.
